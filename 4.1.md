1. Acceleration is constant, starting speed is $V_0$, so
$$V_x = a*t + V_{0}$$

2. The starting position is $X_0$. The change in position is the area under the velocity graph: 

$$X(t) = \int V_x \, dt + X_{0}$$

$$X(t) = \int (a*t + V_{0}) \, dt + X_{0}$$

$$X(t) = \frac{1}{2}*a*t^2+ V_0*t + X_{0}$$
